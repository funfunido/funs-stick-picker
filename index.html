Fun's Stick Picker

First, enter names in the Textbox, 1 for each line. Make sure there are no blank lines.
Then, press ? or / to pick a name. When the name is picked, you can close the window, or eliminate the name.
There are Save buttons 1-5. Click them to save the names currently in the textboxes. Then, click it again at a later time to load the saved names.
There is also a delete save button, to delete all current saves.
